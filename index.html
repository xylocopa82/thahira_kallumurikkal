About Me

I am a multifaceted professional based in the UAE, balancing the worlds of medical science and creative literature. As an alumnus of Salus University (Osborne College of Audiology), I have dedicated my career to diagnosing and treating hearing and balance disorders.

Beyond the clinic, I find my voice in the written word and social activism. Serving as the Medical Wing In-charge of the International Youth Congress (UAE), I strive to bridge the gap between healthcare and community welfare. My journey is one of continuous learning, healing, and telling stories that matter.

Words as a Tool for Change

The Pen is a Scalpel for the Soul

For Dr. Thahira, writing is not a leisure activity—it is a form of social intervention. As the Medical Wing In-charge of the International Youth Congress (UAE), she witnesses the silent struggles of the expatriate community and the youth. She uses her literary voice to:

  • ✓ Address complex social nuances and mental health stigmas.
  • ✓ Empower the youth to find their own voice and identity.
  • ✓ Heal minds through the therapeutic power of storytelling.
